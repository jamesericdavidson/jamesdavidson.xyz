## Human Interest Stories from Across the Globe {#jibberjabberwithjim}

[Jibber Jabber with Jim](/jibberjabberwithjim/) is a podcast where I interview interesting individuals - including subject matter experts on sustainability, human rights, and entrepreneurship.

You can hear new episodes weekly (except for the last Thursday of the month). Every episode is different - take a [sneak peek](/jibberjabberwithjim/#sneakpeek).

### Hear Yourself on British Radio {#appearonfcr}

All it takes is [one message](mailto:james@jamesdavidson.xyz), and you can appear on the [Swing with Jim](/swingwithjim/) show.

How does it work?

1. Own the show

    You choose the topics. You choose the recording date.

    I work around you - you're in control.

2. Deliver positive messages

    Be the change you want to see - inject optimism, hopefulness, and a feel-good factor to your answers.

3. Request your favourite songs

    [FCR Plymouth](https://www.fcradio.co.uk/) listeners hear your interview first.

    It's full-blown radio, and you can request up to three (non-explicit) songs of your choice.
